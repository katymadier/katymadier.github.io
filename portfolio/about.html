About Me

I am a first year Masters student at the U of M School of Information studying Human-Computer Interaction. My design and research interests are in prototyping for augmented and mixed-reality. Additionally, I love conceptualizing solutions to basic human issues through the application of new technologies. After graduation, I hope to work for an organization that helps to provide underserved populations access to technology.

I have a BFA in Painting & Printmaking and experience in a variety of design mediums such as sculpture, woodworking, photography, video, graphic design, UX design, HTML, & CSS. In my spare time, I love reading dystopian sci-fi novels to my daughter and hiking in national parks.
